What is HIB?

HIB stands for Harassment, Intimidation, and Bullying. HIB is mainly common at school or the workplace. HIB usually leads to depression and sometimes even suicide.

  • Learn More

So what is Harassment, Intimidation, and Bullying?

  • Harassment

    Any physical or verbal abuse of a person. Including of her race, religion, age, gender, disability or any other legally protected status.

  • Intimidation

    Intentional behavior that would cause “a person of ordinary sensibilities” fear of injury or harm.

  • Bullying

    The use of force, threats, or coercion to abuse, intimidate, by aggressively dominating over other individuals.

The story of Amanda Todd

On October 10, 2012, Amanda Todd, a Canadian 15 year old, ended her life after suffering for more than two years of harassment, blackmail, and bullying. In 2010, Todd sent out nude photos of her to a stranger online. The photos spread to her school and she was constantly blackmailed and bullyed over it. This caused her to transfer schools but she was still bullyed online. After multiple suicide attempts, she posted a video of her struggle and her story on September 7th, 2012. Unfortunately, the video didn't blow up until after she ended her life.
